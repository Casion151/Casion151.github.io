About Me

Welcome to my personal website. I am Kaixuan Bian, a dedicated researcher with a passion for uncovering the neural underpinnings of conscious perception. My academic journey—from a robust foundation in life sciences to advanced work in neural signal processing and machine learning—drives my commitment to exploring how the brain integrates sensory information.

Research Interests

My research centers on understanding the neural coding schemes that give rise to conscious experience through integrated sensory inputs. I focus on audiovisual integration, speech decoding, and multimodal data analysis using advanced techniques such as spectral-temporal receptive field analysis, cross-subject clustering, and machine learning. I also explore metabolic interactions in bacterial biofilms to draw insights into complex, high-dimensional systems.

I aim to bridge experimental data with computational models by developing novel analytical frameworks that capture the transient yet structured patterns of neural activity, ultimately contributing to a unified model of conscious awareness.
